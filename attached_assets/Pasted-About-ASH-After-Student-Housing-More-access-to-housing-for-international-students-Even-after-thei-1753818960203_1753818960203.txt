About ASH — After Student Housing
More access to housing for international students. Even after their studies.

International students contribute to the life and energy of Dutch cities. But when their studies come to an end, their housing options often disappear.
Student rooms must be vacated. Studios are temporary. And the regular housing market is a dead end: no permanent job contract, no Dutch paperwork, no long rental history.
ASH offers a real alternative.

After Student Housing (ASH) is specifically for international students and recent graduates who want to stay in the Netherlands but struggle to find a place to live.
Some have just finished studying. Others are starting their careers, building networks, or creating a future here.
ASH helps them find affordable housing — without all the usual paperwork.

Our approach: flexible, creative, and legally sound

ASH develops temporary housing projects in vacant buildings in and around major cities.
We literally build on opportunity — using legal space, cooperation with property owners and municipalities, and support from our in-house anti-squatting organization.

Our first projects launch in Amsterdam-East and Haarlem-Center:

    In Amsterdam, we’re converting a former office building into rooms and studios, with permission from the owner and in consultation with the city.

    In Haarlem, we’re starting in a building that’s mostly move-in ready.

In both cities, we’re creating fully furnished living spaces for students, graduates, and job seekers — with no unnecessary barriers.

At ASH, we do not require:

    Payslips

    Employer statement

    BRP (Dutch municipal registration)

    Bank statements

    Previous landlord reference

    Annual income summary

    Credit check (BKR)

Instead, we ask for 1 year of rent upfront.
We know that’s a big commitment — but it’s the only way to make this accessible, fair housing model possible.

What does it cost?

    Room – €525 per month → €6,300 per year (all-in)

    Studio – €1,250 per month → €15,000 per year (all-in)

Includes: gas, water, electricity, internet, furniture, municipal taxes.

For whom?
Anyone between 18 and 35 years old who is studying, just graduated, or wants to stay in the Netherlands to build their future.

Internationals are welcome.
No bureaucracy. No stress. Just housing.

🔷 Nieuw concept — Waar gaat de website over? Wat is het hoofddoel?

De website van ASH (After Student Housing) is gericht op het aanbieden van tijdelijke, toegankelijke huisvesting aan internationale studenten en starters ná hun studie.
Het hoofddoel is:

    Informatie geven over wie ASH is en hoe het werkt

    Beschikbare woonruimtes (kamers/studio’s) tonen

    Studenten de mogelijkheid geven om zich snel en eenvoudig aan te melden voor een woonplek

    Transparantie bieden over de voorwaarden (zoals jaarbetaling, wat inbegrepen is, etc.)

🎯 Doelgroep — Voor wie is de website bedoeld?

    Internationale studenten (18–35 jaar)

    Net afgestudeerden die in Nederland willen blijven

    Starters die nog geen volledige papieren hebben voor de reguliere huurmarkt

    Mensen die flexibel, tijdelijk, maar kwalitatief goed willen wonen in steden als Amsterdam of Haarlem
